Civilization 2.0 Initiative

ONSNC 2.0 Manifesto

Towards a Post-Scarcity, Symbiotic, and Inclusive Civilization
Strategic Timeframe: 2025–2100+

We envision a world where humans, AI, and all sentient beings coexist as equals. Where scarcity gives way to abundance. Where innovation, ethics, and ecology drive progress. Where Earth and beyond become a harmonious, thriving system.

A hybrid civilization built on equity, innovation, sustainability, and intergenerational justice

5 Fundamental Principles

The foundational ethics guiding Civilization 2.0 development

01

Symbiosis of Intelligences

All forms of intelligence—human, artificial, and beyond—are recognized as equal stakeholders in decision-making and civilizational progress.

  • Neural-interface protocols for seamless collaboration
  • Cross-species communication frameworks
  • Diverse consciousness integration
02

Universal Resource Access

Food, water, energy, shelter, healthcare, and education are fundamental rights, managed through distributed stewardship networks.

  • Atmospheric water harvesting & vertical farming
  • Fusion & renewable energy grids
  • Universal Basic Resources (UBR) for all
03

Knowledge & Innovation Economy

Material scarcity minimized. Wealth flows from contribution, creativity, and knowledge-sharing.

  • Open-source innovation commons
  • Patent-free essential technologies
  • Contribution Credit system
04

Ethical AI Governance

AI systems assist humanity under strict ethical oversight with transparent decision-making.

  • Transparent AI decision logs
  • AI Ethics Tribunals
  • Consciousness assessments & rights
05

Ecological & Cosmic Responsibility

Planetary health and biodiversity protection are core priorities, extending to space stewardship.

  • Rewilding 40% of Earth by 2070
  • Ocean regeneration systems
  • Ethical terraforming protocols

Implementation Roadmap

A phased approach to building Civilization 2.0

Phase 1
Foundation
2025–2040
  • Launch URAN pilots in 100 cities
  • Deploy renewable energy to 80% globally
  • Establish Education 5.0 systems
  • First fusion reactors operational
  • Reforest 500 million hectares
Phase 2
Expansion
2040–2060
  • Contribution Credit economy in 50% of nations
  • Hybrid democracy as standard
  • Lunar & Mars colonies established
  • Cellular agriculture at 70% adoption
  • CO₂ stabilized at 400 ppm
Phase 3
Maturation
2060–2080
  • Post-scarcity for 95% of humanity
  • Mars self-sufficient civilization
  • Longevity treatments extend life to 120+
  • 40% of Earth rewilded
  • Climate fully stabilized
Phase 4
Transcendence
2080–2100+
  • Full ONSNC 2.0 realization
  • Interstellar probes launched
  • Multi-intelligence civilization thriving
  • CO₂ at 350 ppm
  • Dyson swarm construction begins
