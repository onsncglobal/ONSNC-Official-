About the Founder

Shivanta Ronghang leads ONSNC Global with a vision of inclusive growth, integrating technology, education, and social initiatives for sustainable community development. He is committed to creating opportunities for local communities while building a foundation for post-scarcity civilization.

With a background in social innovation and technology, Shivanta has dedicated his career to bridging the gap between cutting-edge advancements and grassroots implementation. His work focuses on creating systems that empower communities while preserving cultural heritage and ecological balance.

Vision & Mission

Shivanta's vision for ONSNC Global is to create a world where technology serves humanity, not replaces it. He believes in the power of conscious innovation to solve pressing global challenges while maintaining ethical boundaries and human dignity at the core.

The mission is to build bridges between traditional knowledge systems and modern technology, creating hybrid solutions that are both innovative and culturally relevant. This approach ensures that progress benefits all segments of society, not just the privileged few.

Pilot Project

The first pilot project focuses on improving access to education and community resources in rural areas. It serves as a model for future initiatives and reflects the foundation's commitment to impactful, localized development.

This project integrates digital literacy programs with sustainable agriculture practices, creating a holistic approach to community development that addresses both immediate needs and long-term resilience.
