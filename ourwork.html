What We Do

Education Programs

Supported over 5,000 students in rural areas with digital learning resources.

Community Health

Organized health camps, vaccination drives, and awareness campaigns in underserved regions.

Environmental Initiatives

Planted 50,000+ trees and conducted river clean-up drives across Assam.

Technology & Innovation

Developed local apps and digital tools for education and civic engagement.
