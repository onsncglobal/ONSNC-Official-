🌐 What Are ONSNC Local Chapters?

ONSNC Local Chapters act as decentralized networks for science-driven, community-based innovation. Each chapter connects educators, researchers, technologists, and local citizens to implement sustainable transformation at the grassroots level.

🤝 Community Engagement

Collaborate with regional leaders and mobilize youth for social impact projects.

💡 Innovation Hubs

Enable local problem-solving through evidence-based experiments and prototypes.

🚀 Leadership Development

Train as a local coordinator representing ONSNC’s Civilization 2.0 mission.

📋 Start a Local Chapter

Fill out a short application form to propose your regional chapter. Once reviewed, our team will contact you for onboarding and collaboration.

Application Process

  1. Complete the Google Form application.
  2. Our review team evaluates within 5 – 7 working days.
  3. Selected applicants receive approval and chapter kit.
