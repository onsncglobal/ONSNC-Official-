Our Vision for Humanity

ONSNC 30 Sustainable Civilization Goals

A comprehensive framework to guide humanity toward an equitable, sustainable, and thriving civilization for all.

Progress toward achieving the SCGs

1

Inequality Annihilation

Build a civilization where inequality is structurally impossible through systemic redesign.

Social Equity Priority: Critical
2

Universal Basic Resource Access

Food, water, shelter, energy, healthcare, and digital access as guaranteed rights for all.

Social Equity Priority: Critical
3

Ecological Restoration

Reverse environmental degradation and restore biodiversity through global conservation efforts.

Environmental Priority: High
4

Circular Economy

Transition to a zero-waste economic model where all resources are reused and regenerated.

Economic Priority: High
5

Participatory Governance

Establish transparent, inclusive decision-making systems with citizen participation at all levels.

Governance Priority: Medium
6

Universal Education

Lifelong learning opportunities accessible to all, fostering critical thinking and creativity.

Social Equity Priority: High
